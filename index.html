Why Nucleus

Nucleus is an online community where Programming Students, Alumni and Employers meet. We fill the gap between the lack of programmers in South Florida and job seekers.

Learning Never Ends

Not only is technology always changing. It’s also important to remember that the internet has a numerous amount of recourses to learn from as a programmer. Being a part of the online community as programming students, and recent programming graduates; this is a platform where likeminded people share resources that helped them learn and grow. With a numerous amount of information shared on this one platform, as a one stop shop to find resources that you need; as a student programmer entering the world as a professional. At SoFlo Nucleus learning never ends.

Networking is Key

A programmer can have a great amount of knowledge. But the main key how many people and companies are you speaking to face to face. We know the internet is great. There are somethings that should be done in person to reach a goal. Like networking. We share events that are amazing for student programmers to go for networking purposes. Shaking a hand and looking someone in eye is important. Networking is important for those who would like to succeed as programmers. So, let’s get up from behind the computer and shake some hands.

Professionalism

Sometimes as programming students’ professionalism kind of falls by the waste side and is forgotten. If you want to work for yourself or with a company it is important to remain professional always. Stay humble, do what you promise, learn to work together with a team, treat everyone with respect and learn the soft skills that companies want. You will go far. At SoFlo professionalism should never be forgotten.
